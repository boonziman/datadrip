### Unpacking the ChatGPT Backlash: From Privacy Nightmares to Performance Pitfalls

The cracks in ChatGPT's armor have been widening for years, but 2026 has seen them fracture into full-blown crises. Fast-forward to this year, and the scandals have escalated.
