## How to play LetterPop

1. You get a daily pool of **20 letter tiles**. Three are power-ups.
2. Tap tiles in order to spell a word (3 letters or more), then **Submit**.
3. Valid words consume those tiles. Used tiles can't be reused.
4. Power-up tiles:
   - **★ Wild** — counts as any letter you pick.
   - **2L** — that letter's value is doubled.
   - **2W** — the entire word's score is doubled.
5. Longer words score bonus points (+5 per letter over 4).
6. Clear all 20 tiles for the **+50 GO-OUT bonus**.
7. One pool per day. Everyone in the world gets the same letters.

## Scoring

Each letter is worth its Scrabble-style value (A/E/I = 1, Q/Z = 10, etc.).
Stack power-ups for huge scores: a 2W + 2L on a 7-letter word can pop 60+ points in a single play.

## FAQ

**Is LetterPop free?** Yes. Free, no sign-up, no ads in the game UI.
**How is the daily pool generated?** Deterministically from the date — same 20 tiles for every player worldwide.
**Can I play multiple times a day?** No. One pool per day, locked once you finish.
