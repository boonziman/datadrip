## How to play Spelling Bee

1. Tap the **🔊 speaker** to hear today's word.
2. Type your spelling and submit.
3. Stuck? Tap **📖 Definition** or **💬 Used in sentence** for hints.
4. Five rounds per day, increasing in difficulty from easy → expert.
5. Score 3+ to extend your streak. Score 5/5 for a perfect day.
6. Audio is sourced from the free public Dictionary API and falls back to your device's text-to-speech voice.
