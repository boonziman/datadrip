## How to play Songless

1. Press **▶** to hear a 1-second snippet of today's mystery song.
2. Type your guess, or tap **⏭ Skip** to unlock a longer snippet.
3. You get six chances. The snippet grows: 1s → 2s → 4s → 7s → 11s → 16s.
4. Right title = win. Right artist (wrong title) = a partial hint.
5. After the game, play the full 30-second preview.
6. Audio previews are streamed live from the free **Apple Music / iTunes Search** preview catalog.
