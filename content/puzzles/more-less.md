## How to play More/less

1. Each round shows two real items from a category (YouTube subs, Spotify listeners, market caps, city populations, crypto, box office…).
2. Tap the side you think has the **higher** value.
3. Right answer → next round.
4. Wrong answer → **run is over**. The longer your streak, the better your daily score.
5. Survive all 15 rounds for a perfect run.
6. New rounds and categories every day.
