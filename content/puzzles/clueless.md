## How to play Clueless

1. There's one secret word. You have unlimited guesses.
2. Every guess is ranked by how **semantically related** it is to the target.
3. The colored bar shows how close you are:
   - 🎯 **FOUND** — you got it!
   - 🔥 **hot** — top 50, very close.
   - 🌡 **warm** — top 200.
   - ❄ **cool** — top 500.
   - **cold** — not in the top 1,000 closest words.
4. Stuck? Tap **💡 Hint** to be given the next-closest word you haven't tried.
5. Tap **🏳 Give up** to reveal today's word.
6. Powered by the free [Datamuse API](https://www.datamuse.com/api/) — your guesses stay on your device.
