My analysis? This could be Ethereum’s moonshot. If successful, it positions ETH as the backbone for AI-driven Web3 apps—think decentralized AI agents managing trades without centralized servers. But risks abound: Implementation delays could exacerbate volatility, especially if AI psychosis leads to broader tech sell-offs. Opportunities for readers? Stake in ETH now, before the upgrade hype builds. I’ve followed Vitalik’s roadmaps since 2017, and this feels like his most pragmatic yet—less pie-in-the-sky, more engineered for 2026’s realities.
